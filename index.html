Selling Platforms

Where and how to sell

Depop

Young buyers, easy to list, photo-focused, and strong for fashion categories.

OfferUp

Useful for local sales and faster meetups when done safely and legally.

Facebook Marketplace

Large audience and broad demand for many product types.

Listing tips that improve trust

  • Take clear photos in good lighting
  • Show tags, flaws, and condition honestly
  • Write simple descriptions with measurements
  • Set competitive prices after checking comps
  • Respond to buyers quickly and professionally
  • Ship on time and track every sale

Pricing tips

Check sold listings, account for platform fees, shipping, packaging, and your time before setting your price.

Photo tips

Use daylight or soft light, clean backgrounds, and front/back/detail shots to improve trust and conversion.

FAQ

Common questions

Do I need experience?

No. Beginners can follow a structured process and learn as they go.

How much money do I need to start?

You can start small, but costs vary depending on product type, shipping, and fees.

How fast can items sell?

Some sell in days, others take longer depending on demand, pricing, and condition.

Can I make my first sale quickly?

It is possible, but results are never guaranteed. Consistency matters most.
